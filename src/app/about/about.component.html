About Us

NSBE's mission is "to increase the number of culturally responsible black engineers who excel academically, succeed professionally and positively impact the community." The National Society of Black Engineers (NSBE) is a 501(C)(3) non-profit association that is owned and managed by its members. Beyond looking to develop adult, we believe that the future of technology is in the ends of our youths; this is why we have also venture in a journey to engage, mentor, coach and develop our youth of Portland who shows interest in science, engineering and math. Now, let's make our world a technological advanced place!
